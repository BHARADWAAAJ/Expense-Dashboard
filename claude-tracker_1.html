About Penny

A calm little money companion. Everything stays on your device — no accounts, no tracking, works offline. Penny is an independent app and isn't affiliated with Anthropic or Claude.

Tip: just talk to Penny in the Chat tab. Try "spent 240 on coffee" or "got 50000 salary".
